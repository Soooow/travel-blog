Travel Tips

  1. Cultural Awareness: India's rich cultural diversity demands respect for local customs and traditions. Covering your shoulders and knees is advisable when visiting religious sites.
    2.  
  2. Food and Water: Embrace the flavors of Indian cuisine, but exercise caution with street food to avoid digestive issues. Stick to bottled water and skip ice in drinks to stay healthy.
    3.  
  3. Health Precautions: Consult a doctor before your trip for vaccinations and health advice. Pack essential medications, sunscreen, insect repellent, and a basic first aid kit.
    4.  
  4. Bargaining: Engage in friendly bargaining when shopping in markets. It's expected and can be an enjoyable way to interact with local vendors.
    5.  
  5. Transportation: Choose trains for longer journeys to experience the countryside. Urban areas offer ride-sharing apps and pre-paid taxis for local travel.
    6.  
  6. Language: While English is prevalent, learning a few basic Hindi phrases can enhance your experience and foster connections with locals.
    7.  
  7. Currency: Carry both cash and cards. Inform your bank about your travel plans to avoid card issues. Exchange currency at authorized outlets for the best rates.
    8.  
  8. Safety: Remain vigilant of your belongings in crowded areas. Research destinations, follow local advice, and stay aware of surroundings for a secure journey.
    9.
